About

Numbers first, story second.

The short version of how I work — and where I learned to do it.

I'm a data analyst who likes problems that come with too many rows and not enough answers yet. Whether it's half a million insurance policies or a messy sales export, I start with the same question: what does this data actually prove, and what does it just imply?

My background spans mathematics, statistics, and computing — which means I'm as comfortable writing a query as I am explaining a trend to someone who's never opened Power BI. I'm looking for analyst roles where that combination is useful.

Motor Insurance Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard analysing a motor insurer's financial performance from 2014–2018 across 508,000 policies. Surfaced a premium-to-claim ratio of just 44%, a sharp Q2 2018 spike suggesting a market exit or repricing, and Toyota, Isuzu, and Iveco as the top claim drivers — while motorcycles led in policy volume despite running at a loss.
